ABOUT

Are you brave enough to enter the room?.
SMART CODING is an event of TECSTASY 2k18 organized as a part of SUDHEE by IT department of CBIT. A coding competition where contestants are trapped in a room for a certain amount of time in which they will be given a mix of logical reasoning and programming based questions.The trick here is to find the answer to get the next clue.Each step will get you and your team closer to the key.Find the key by answering all the questions before time runs out!.
All participants will be rewarded and the top scorers will be given merit certificates apart from cash prizes.
So are you up for the challenge ?

Instructions

Participants must be individuals or a team of two.
A set of questions will be given which are required to be solved in a given time.
Questions will be a mix of logical reasoning and programming questions.
Solutions to a set of questions will be clue for the next one.
If you are stuck at some question, you can take hints by paying extra 10Rs/- per hint.
Answers will be graded and the winner will be decided on the basis of score and time taken.
