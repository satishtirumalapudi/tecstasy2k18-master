ABOUT

WEB KOOKS is an event of TECSTASY 2k18 organized as a part of SUDHEE by IT department of CBIT. All you need to do is submit your User Interface (UI), along with a Powerpoint presentation. Our technical team will check your UI for syntax and plagiarism (< 30%). The team that makes best design wins and shall be rewarded with cash prize and a merit certificate. So, what are you waiting for! Come, participate and give us the best you can. See you all there!
